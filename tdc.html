2018 Tour De Colorado

The Tour De Colorado is a series of tournaments through out Colorado, aimed to crown the top 8 players in the state. While tournaments are open to any team, only teams from Colorado can earn points.

This year, we are focusing on individual rankings. Each teammate will split the points earned from a tournament placement. For every tournament you play with a new teammate, five points will be added to your individual score.

Last year's top four teams each won trophies and a fully sublimated, custom Rocky Mountain Roundnet Elite jersey. These jerseys were provided by our title sponsor: Savage Apparel. Prizes for 2018 have yet to be determined, but will be awesome.
