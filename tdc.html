2018 Tour De Colorado



The TDC is a series of tournaments throughout the state of Colorado aimed to find the best teams and players in the state.
In 2017 we found the 4 best teams, in 2018 we found the top 5 players.

This year we will be moving back to the team format but will still be keeping individual stats.
We will consistently be updating the top 25 players in Colorado, throughout the series
