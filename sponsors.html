Sponsors

Interested in being a sponsor for the 2018 season? We are currently looking for sponsors for the upcoming 2018 Tour De Colorado and the RMR League sessions.

Sponsorships can include anything from monetary donations which would help cover costs like field permits, refreshments, prize pools, etc., to product donations for raffles, giveaways, and/or prizes.

We would promote your company through social media, newsletters, and flyers. Your logo will be displayed on our website along with any apparel that get printed for our events. We are also open to any ideas you may have.
