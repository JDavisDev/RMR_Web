Sponsors







Interested in being a sponsor for the 2018 season? We are currently looking for sponsors for the upcoming 2018 Tour De Colorado and the RMR League sessions.

Sponsorships can include anything from monetary donations which would help cover costs like field permits, refreshments, prize pools, etc., to product donations for raffles, giveaways, and/or prizes.

We would promote your company through social media, newsletters, and flyers. Your logo will be displayed on our website along with any apparel that get printed for our events. We are also open to any ideas you may have.

The TDC is a series of 6 roundnet (Spikeball) tournaments across Colorado to determine the top 5 ballers in the state. Although these tournaments are open to teams from all across the globe, only players that reside in Colorado are eligible to be deemed “Rocky Mountain Roundnet Elite” and win custom, fully sublimated jerseys.

This year we will have 6 tournaments from January - September. The dates are as follows:

1/20 - Boulder
4/28 - Denver
5/26 - Fort Collins
June - Vail/Golden (date/location not yet finalized)
7/28 - Co Springs (part of the 2018 Rocky Mountain State Games)
9/29 - Denver (grand slam event - 6th annual Mile High Classic - date not yet finalized)
